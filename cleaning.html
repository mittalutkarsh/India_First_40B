Data Cleaning

B.2 · Objective-driven filtering

Cleaning strategy: every stage of the pipeline will serve a defined objective rather than generic hygiene. We will build on AI4Bharat's Setu, a Spark-based pipeline for extraction, cleaning, filtering, and deduplication, and extend it for provenance integrity.

  • Family-aware language identification — script-sharing languages (Hindi and Marathi both use Devanagari) will be separated with a language-family detector (IndicLID); a mislabelled document corrupts both languages' counts and the per-language repetition budget.
  • Conservative script normalization — Indic scripts encode the same grapheme several ways; we will canonicalize the encoding without stripping meaningful diacritics or dependent vowels, since over-normalization inflates fertility and damages morphology.
  • Perplexity-based quality filtering — a CCNet-style n-gram model trained on verified text will score each document; high perplexity flags garbled or mislabelled content.
  • Deduplication (exact, fuzzy, cross-tier) — MinHash and SemDedup will remove near-duplicates; cross-tier duplicates will be dropped so the provenance tiers stay disjoint. This keeps the repetition accounting valid, since undetected duplicates would push effective epochs past the ~4-epoch cap.
  • Translationese filtering — machine-translated text shows lower type-token ratio and lower unigram entropy (Velasco & Roque, 2025); a document will enter a native tier only if its translationese score is low, otherwise it is treated as translated. This keeps the native tiers native.
  • Synthetic-data gating — synthetic text will be generated once, mixed with real data, and never fed back recursively; bland or ungrounded generations will be rejected or demoted to translated.
  • Toxicity removal — Setu toxicity filtering with Indic word-lists.
  • Decontamination and perspective-corpus hold-out — evaluation text will be removed and canary strings planted; the contrastive perspective corpus will be strictly excluded from pretraining, so separability measures a learned vantage rather than memorization.
  • Chauvinism guarding — hostile, us-versus-them framing will be removed from the tiers we repeat and from the chosen side of preference pairs, admitted only as the rejected_B example.
Ordering will be enforced

Deduplication precedes repetition accounting; translationese filtering precedes native-tier trust; decontamination precedes training.
